Privacy Policy for Chargio-X

Last updated: January 2025

Chargio-X, developed by BrizerHero, is a utility application that helps users monitor their phone's battery charging status. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

Chargio-X does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app only reads the following device information locally, within your phone:

  • Battery percentage
  • Charging/unplugged status

Users may enter a custom spoken message for full-charge notifications. This message is saved only on the user's device and is never sent to any server.

2. No Personal Data Collection

The app does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Use analytics or tracking
  • Use ads
  • Share data with any third parties

3. Permissions Used

  • Foreground/Background Service: Required to run charging alerts while the app is closed.
  • Battery Status Access: Used to read battery level and charging updates.

No permission gives the app access to personal files, photos, contacts, or location.

4. How We Use Information

Battery status is used solely for:

  • Charging milestone alerts (20%, 40%, 60%, 80%)
  • Full-charge spoken alerts, repeated up to 12 times
  • Battery health reminders

5. Children's Privacy

The app is safe for users of all ages and does not collect personal data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted on this page.
