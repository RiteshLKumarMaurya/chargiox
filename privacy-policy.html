ChargIO (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information. By using ChargIO, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

ChargIO does not collect or store any personal data. The app works completely offline on your device.

The app only accesses limited system information required for functionality:

  • Battery Level & Status: Used to speak battery percentage, charging, disconnect, and full-charge alerts.
  • Notification Permission (Android 13+): Required for foreground service notifications.
  • Battery Optimization / Auto-start Settings: Required on some devices to allow background monitoring.
  • Custom Message Input: Saved only locally on your device.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

ChargIO does NOT collect any of the following:

  • Personal information
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Location data
  • Photos, videos, or files
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Advertising ID, etc.)
  • Usage analytics
  • Any data sent to remote servers

No information leaves your device at any time.

3. How the App Uses Information

The limited data accessed is used only for:

  • Monitoring battery level
  • Speaking alerts and notifications
  • Running the charging protection service

We do not use your data for advertising, tracking, or selling to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

ChargIO shares no data with:

  • Third parties
  • Advertisers
  • Servers or cloud services

No data is transmitted outside your device.

5. Security

Because ChargIO stores no personal data and uses no servers, your privacy remains fully protected at all times.

6. Third-Party Services

ChargIO does not integrate analytics, ads, or SDKs. The only optional external action is opening WhatsApp when the user taps "Contact Us". No personal information is ever sent from the app.

7. Children’s Privacy

ChargIO does not collect any data and is safe for users of all ages.
